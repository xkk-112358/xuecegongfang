# Xuece Workshop User Manual

## Platform Introduction

Xuece Workshop is a convenient online platform for exercise practice, self-testing and question bank management and sharing. It supports uploading various document-based question banks and automatically splits questions. You can answer questions online quickly and get automatic scoring. It also allows easy sharing of question banks. Perfect for daily study, knowledge check and group practice with simple operations.

## Core Functions

### Multi-format Question Bank Upload

Support uploading Word and other document files. There is no need to input questions manually. Directly upload sorted exercises to save plenty of time.

### Automatic Question Extraction

After uploading, the platform can automatically identify and separate questions, options and answers. You can finish importing with one click and get usable question banks right away.

### Easy Question Bank Sharing

You can share your uploaded question banks with classmates and friends freely. Others can use them directly without repeated uploading to realize resource sharing.

### Question Bank Management

You can check all imported complete question banks and view all questions clearly. It helps you manage personal study materials and practice in classified ways.

### Simple Operation Steps

1.Access the platform
  Open your browser and enter the homepage of Xuece Workshop. All functions are easy to find and use.

2.Upload question banks
  Click the upload button on the homepage
  Select local exercise files including multiple-choice questions and fill-in-the-blank questions
  Confirm uploading and wait for automatic extraction until it succeeds

3.Share question banks
Enter the sharing page
Choose the question bank you want to share to generate links or passwords
Send them to others, and they can start practicing immediately

### Notes

Arrange uploaded questions in neat layout with clear answers for higher recognition accuracy.Make sure the platform runs normally when sharing resources.Do not close the page casually during answering to prevent data loss.All resources are only for personal study. Do not upload illegal or copyrighted content.Even beginners can master it easily.
